Our Story

What Is FlavorPop?

FlavorPop is a campus-based startup born from a simple observation: university students are constantly choosing between taste, health, and affordability — and the current options force them to compromise on at least one. We set out to change that.

We produce handcrafted flavoured popcorn made from whole-grain kernels, packaged in eco-friendly brown paper bags, and sold at just PKR 100 — accessible to every student and faculty member on campus.
